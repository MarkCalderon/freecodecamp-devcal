Header

This is typically used for adding title or subtitles in a website.

                        <h1>Hello World</h1>

They vary in different sizes for example:

                        <h2>Hello World</h2>
                        <h3>Hello World</h3>
                        <h4>Hello World</h4>
                        <h5>Hello World</h5>

Unordered List

This is typically used for keeping a collection of items.

Brand of shoes
  • Nika
  • Underamor
  • Addides
  • Cheetoh
                        <h5>Brand of shoes</h5>
                        <ul>

                        </ul>
